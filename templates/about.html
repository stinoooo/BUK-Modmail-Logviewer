About BUK's Logviewer

A Modern Solution for Moderation Transparency
Our Purpose

BUK's Logviewer is crafted to ensure transparency and accountability within our community's moderation efforts. By providing an easy-to-use interface for reviewing Modmail transcripts, the Moderation Team can efficiently manage and resolve user inquiries with clarity and professionalism.

Features
  • Secure storage of Modmail transcripts
  • Search and filter functionalities
  • User-friendly interface designed for efficient moderation
  • Direct integration with BUK's Discord server
Our Vision

We envision a moderation environment where every inquiry is managed swiftly and fairly, and where every team member has access to the tools they need to maintain a positive and healthy community atmosphere. The BUK Logviewer plays a pivotal role in this, as we strive to set a new standard in Discord moderation tools.

Built by bossman

Developed exclusively for the BUK Moderation Team, this tool represents the next step in enhancing moderation effectiveness while preserving user trust. We are committed to continual improvements, ensuring the system meets the evolving needs of our community.
